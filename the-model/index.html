What RAM Does

One model. A single, transparent environment.

RAM demonstrates how competition, evaluation, and award can occur in one transparent, data-driven environment that delivers capabilities to the field faster. It brings together commercial-first access, measurable performance, and accelerated delivery into a single model.

A Different Starting Point

Less paperwork upfront. More direct understanding of the solution.

Through concise, video-based submissions, innovators present five to seven minute solution pitches reviewed against published evaluation criteria. This replaces lengthy, paperwork-heavy cycles with a more direct, transparent process — government reviewers gain a clearer view of the technology, the team, the use case, and the mission fit before moving into deeper engagement.
