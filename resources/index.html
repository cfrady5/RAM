Submission FAQ

Common questions about submitting.

What is required for a submission?

Organization details, a capability description, technology area, target government use case, solution maturity, a 5–7 minute video pitch, and supporting documentation.

Who can submit?

Companies, startups, research teams, academic institutions, and non-traditional vendors seeking a clearer pathway into government acquisition.

How are submissions reviewed?

Submissions may be reviewed against published evaluation criteria with subject-matter expert input and mission-focused assessment.

Where do documents live?

Key documents and guides are listed in the Documents & Guides section above. Some materials require an access request through the Contact Us page.

Who should I contact with questions?

Reach the RAM team through the Contact Us page and select the inquiry type that best fits your question.
